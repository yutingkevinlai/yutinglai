I am a Graduate Student Researcher in the Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory (MacLab) at the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department, UCLA, Los Angeles, USA.

Before joining MacLab, I was an Assistant Software Engineer in the Self-Driving Car Group at ITRI, Taiwan working together with Prof. Chieh-Chih Wang, where I worked on the topic of Traffic Light Detection and Lidar Camera Sensor Fusion.
