Welcome

I am a Graduate Student Researcher in the Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory (MacLab) at the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department, UCLA, Los Angeles, USA.

Before joining MacLab, I was an Assistant Engineer in the Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory at UCLA working together with Prof. Tsu-chin Tsao, where I obtained my PhD on the topic of an Optical Coherence Tomography–Guided Robotic System for Automated Retinal Microsurgery.

My research includes:

  • Design, manufacture, and control of robotic systems
  • Intraocular robotic surgical systems and automation
  • Image processing of medical imagery including optical coherence tomography (OCT) data
