Welcome

I am a Graduate Student Researcher in the Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory (MacLab) at the Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department, UCLA, Los Angeles, USA.

Before joining MacLab, I was an Assistant Software Engineer in the Mechatronics and Mechanical System Laboratory at ITRI (Industrial Technology Research Institute) working together with Prof. Chieh-chih Wang, where I worked on the topic of Traffic Light Detection and Sensor Fusion in Autonomous Vehicle.
